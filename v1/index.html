Fitzhugh-Nagumo neuron pairs

This shows 2 pairs of Fitzhugh-Nagumo model neurons. The left pair has a mutually excitatory relationship, and the right pair has a mutually inhibitory one.

Static model parameters are chosen so each neuron exhibits stable spiking oscillations (without user input). Both pairs exhibit stable phase coupling, with the left excitatory pair being synchronous and the right inhibitory pair anti-synchronous.

Keys: Click and hold anywhere in the canvas area to inhibit the top neuron of each pair. Both pairs return to their stable phase-coupled synchrony (or anti-synchrony) after releasing the mouse button.
